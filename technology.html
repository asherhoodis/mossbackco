Technology

Technology is embedded across the lifecycle: sourcing → diligence → operations.

Our Technology Stack

Data

Public records, market data, proprietary datasets. We aggregate information from multiple sources to build comprehensive market intelligence and identify opportunities before they're widely known.

Models

Valuation engines, risk assessment, pattern recognition. Machine learning models process raw data to surface insights—from property valuations to acquisition target clustering to market trend forecasting.

Automation

Workflow orchestration, alerts, document processing. Automated systems handle routine tasks—deal monitoring, due diligence checklists, document extraction, and operational reporting—freeing our team to focus on judgment and strategy.

Visualization

Dashboards, reporting, performance tracking. Clean, actionable interfaces display portfolio performance, deal pipelines, and operational metrics. We design dashboards that make complex data accessible and drive better decisions.

Technology Across the Lifecycle

1. Sourcing

We monitor thousands of properties, businesses, and data points daily. Custom scrapers track listings, public filings, and transaction records. Machine learning flags opportunities matching our investment criteria. Automated alerts notify us the moment something interesting appears.

  • • Web scraping and data aggregation
  • • Pattern matching and opportunity scoring
  • • Real-time alerts and deal flow monitoring

2. Diligence

Once we identify a target, automation accelerates analysis. Financial models pull data directly from property management systems and accounting software. AI reviews lease agreements, contracts, and environmental reports. Computer vision analyzes property condition. We can run comprehensive diligence in a fraction of traditional timelines.

  • • Automated financial analysis and modeling
  • • Document extraction and contract review
  • • Property condition assessment via computer vision

3. Operations

Post-acquisition, technology drives performance improvement. For real estate: automated rent collection, predictive maintenance scheduling, and dynamic pricing. For businesses: workflow automation, centralized reporting, and performance dashboards. Our portfolio companies inherit our technology infrastructure immediately.

  • • Automated operations and maintenance
  • • KPI dashboards and performance tracking
  • • Predictive analytics and optimization

Our Approach

We're pragmatic about technology. It's a tool, not a religion. Off-the-shelf software works for most problems. But where competitive advantage matters—proprietary deal sourcing, custom analytics, unique workflows—we build in-house.

Our team includes developers, data scientists, and domain experts who can turn investment insights into production code. We move fast, iterate constantly, and measure results. Technology serves strategy, not the other way around.
