Investment Focus

We deploy capital across three complementary areas, each enhanced by data and technology.

Real Estate & Infrastructure

Institutional underwriting. Geographic edge. Durable cash flows.

Focus on Texas & Sun Belt

We concentrate investments in markets we know deeply. Texas and the Sun Belt offer population growth, business migration, and infrastructure gaps that create opportunity. Our geographic focus gives us sourcing advantages and local intelligence that national players miss.

Underwriting Discipline

Every deal undergoes rigorous financial analysis and scenario modeling. We stress test assumptions across interest rate environments, occupancy levels, and economic cycles. Our models incorporate property-level cash flows, market comparables, and risk-adjusted returns. We only move forward when the numbers work.

Asset Classes

  • • Multifamily properties with value-add potential
  • • Industrial and logistics facilities
  • • Infrastructure projects with contracted revenue
  • • Land development in high-growth corridors

Private Equity & Roll-Ups

Operational excellence and tech-enabled integration.

Silver Tsunami Opportunities

Millions of baby boomers own profitable small businesses with no succession plan. These companies have customers, cash flow, and proven business models—but lack institutional infrastructure. We acquire founder-led businesses and professionalize operations while preserving what works.

Integration Playbooks

Consolidation only creates value if executed well. We've built detailed playbooks for integrating acquisitions: standardizing back-office functions, implementing KPI dashboards, centralizing procurement, and cross-selling services. Technology accelerates integration—CRM systems, accounting automation, and operational analytics turn fragmented businesses into scalable platforms.

Target Sectors

  • • Essential services with recurring revenue
  • • B2B services with high customer retention
  • • Regional businesses ready for multi-market expansion
  • • Industries ripe for technology-driven consolidation

AI & Automation

Proprietary analytics for sourcing, diligence, and operations.

Deal Sourcing & Market Intelligence

We build custom tools to monitor markets, identify off-market opportunities, and track competitor activity. Web scraping, public records analysis, and pattern recognition help us find deals before they hit the market. Our systems surface opportunities that manual processes would miss.

Due Diligence Acceleration

AI speeds up due diligence without sacrificing quality. We use machine learning for document review, financial analysis automation, and risk flagging. Natural language processing extracts key terms from leases and contracts. Computer vision analyzes property condition from drone footage and satellite imagery. What used to take weeks now takes days.

Operational Optimization

Once we own an asset, technology drives performance. For real estate: predictive maintenance reduces downtime, automated lease administration cuts overhead, and dynamic pricing maximizes revenue. For operating businesses: workflow automation frees up staff, data dashboards surface inefficiencies, and forecasting models improve capital allocation.

Build vs. Buy

We're pragmatic about technology. Off-the-shelf solutions work for most problems. But where competitive advantage matters—deal sourcing, custom analytics, proprietary workflows—we build in-house. Our team includes developers, data scientists, and domain experts who can turn investment insights into production code.
